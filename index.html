Our mission:

Welcome to China By The Sea - A family owned and operated establishment since the 1980s with a passion for great food and service all in a pleasant atmosphere. Thanks for visiting our page and we hope to see you at our restaurant in Boothbay Harbor, Maine.

We have been open in town for many years providing to our community satisfying Asian cuisine in a relaxing environment. Located in the heart of Boothbay Harbor, we have been offering the most authentic and elegant Chinese Cuisine experience possible since 1980.

Our goal here at China By The Sea is to provide lovers of Chinese Food with authentic and tasty Chinese cuisine and at the same time, provide food that is truly good for you. We also offer a lighter menu featuring tasty steamed dishes prepared without salt, cornstarch, or MSG.

We take pride in preparing dishes that delight all the senses and would love for you to experience the best Chinese Cuisine the Boothbay area has to offer.

Customer Reviews

“I work down the road and probably eat here about once a month (either take-out or a quick lunch with coworkers). Their food is consistently solid, and as far as small town Chinese places go, this is the best I've ever had. There's never any fat or gristle on the meats (which I find you run into all the time at Chinese restaurants) and their prices are very reasonable. All of the appetizers that I've tried have been great, and I really enjoy their chicken with spicy garlic sauce and chicken and broccoli. I have a friend that swears by their soup too.

Also, oddly enough my husband and I both agree that their french fries are amazing (I know - not what you'd think your go-to would be at a Chinese restaurant. I'd highly recommend trying them - they're addictive.”

Adria K.

Bath, ME
